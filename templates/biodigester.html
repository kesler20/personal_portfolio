Biodigester Hackathon

For an Hackathon organised by The Green Impact we had to design a biodigester that would use the organic waste of the Diamond building at the University of Sheffield to produce methane

Our team to integrated an Air flow mixing technique that would maximise mass transfer between methanogenic bacteria and the waste

The technology was researched by a pHD student
